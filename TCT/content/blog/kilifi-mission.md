Thika County is home to over 1.4 million people. A significant proportion of our neighbours have never heard the gospel presented clearly. Many have heard *about* Jesus — but a name is not the same as a living, transforming relationship.

TC Thika exists to change that.

## The W.I.D.E Vision

Our mission to *Reach W.I.D.E for Christ* is structured around four concrete commitments:

**W — Witness** to 100,000 people with the good news of Jesus Christ. We believe that every member of TC Thika is a missionary.

**I — Impact** six sectors of society: family, government, business, education, arts, and media. The gospel is not privatised — it transforms culture.

**D — Discipleship** of 10,000 people outside the Chapel walls. We want to see communities established where people are growing in Christ, not just attending services.

**E — Establish Churches** in Malindi and Vipingo. The goal is not one megachurch — it is a movement of multiplying communities.

## What This Looks Like in Practice

Every week, members of TC Thika are having conversations with neighbours, colleagues, and family members. Every Tuesday there are outreach teams active in Mnarani. Every year we support church-planting initiatives across the coast.

If you want to be part of the mission, start close. Pray for five people you know who do not yet know Jesus. Then tell us — we will help you take the next step.

The city needs the gospel. And God has placed us here for exactly this moment.
